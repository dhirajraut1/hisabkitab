Our Promise

Effective 15 April 2026 5 min read 100% Offline App

Hisab Kitab was built with a single guiding principle: your financial data belongs to you, and only you. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your personal or financial information. Everything you enter into the app — your income, expenses, assets, and budgets — stays on your device, always.

01

Overview

The short version of everything

This Privacy Policy describes how Hisab Kitab ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application. Hisab Kitab is a fully offline personal finance manager designed for users in Nepal, supporting both Bikram Sambat (BS) and Gregorian (AD) calendar systems.

📵 No internet required 🔒 No account needed 🚫 No ads, ever 📤 No data leaves your phone 🏦 No third-party analytics 💾 Local storage only

By installing and using Hisab Kitab, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you disagree with any part, you may uninstall the application at any time.

02

Data We Collect

What the app knows about you

Hisab Kitab stores data locally on your device only. The following information is saved to your phone's internal storage when you use the app:

Data Type Examples Purpose
Transaction Records Amount, category, date, notes, payment mode Track income & expenses
Asset Information Bank accounts, wallet balances, share values Net worth tracking
Budget Data Category limits, periods, targets Budget management
Custom Categories User-defined category names, icons, colors Personalisation
Split Groups Group names, member names, shared expenses Expense splitting
App Settings Calendar preference (BS/AD), currency setting User experience

Important: None of this data is ever transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. It exists only within the app's local storage on your personal device.

03

How Data is Stored

Where your information lives

All data entered into Hisab Kitab is stored using Android's AsyncStorage — a local, sandboxed storage system on your device. This storage is:

  • Accessible only by the Hisab Kitab application — no other app can read it
  • Stored entirely on your phone's internal memory
  • Deleted automatically when you uninstall the application
  • Never synced to any cloud service (Google Drive, iCloud, etc.) unless you explicitly back up your phone
  • Protected by your device's own security (PIN, fingerprint, encryption)

We recommend enabling device-level encryption (available in Android Settings → Security) for an additional layer of protection on all your locally stored data.

04

Data Sharing

Who else can see your data

We do not share your data with anyone — because we never receive it in the first place. The following entities have zero access to your Hisab Kitab data:

  • Hisab Kitab developers and team members
  • Advertising networks or marketing companies
  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Government agencies or law enforcement (we have nothing to provide)
  • Payment processors or financial institutions
  • Any other third party, under any circumstance

No internet connection is required to use Hisab Kitab. The app works entirely offline. This means there is no technical mechanism by which your data could be sent anywhere — even unintentionally.

Your Rights

What you are always entitled to

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete and total control at all times. You may exercise the following rights directly within the app or your device — no requests to us required:

  • Right to Access: View all your data at any time within the app
  • Right to Correct: Edit any transaction, asset, or budget directly in the app
  • Right to Delete: Delete individual records, or uninstall the app to remove everything
  • Right to Portability: Export your data via the app's backup feature
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Simply stop using the app — no data is processed outside your device

Since we do not collect, store, or process your data on any external system, there is no need to submit a formal data access or deletion request to us. Your control is immediate and absolute.

Children's Privacy

Use by minors

Hisab Kitab does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The application is intended for general audiences and designed primarily for adults managing personal finances.

Since the app collects no data whatsoever on external servers, it poses no data privacy risk to users of any age. However, we recommend parental guidance for younger users interacting with financial tools.
